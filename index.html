01 / Positioning

Staking a claim in the AI era.

My work sits between business systems, digital design, AI tooling, creative strategy, and cultural analysis. I am interested in how ideas become structured products, interfaces, stories, and workflows.

Because AI is still an emerging professional space, the goal is not to wait until every role is clearly defined. The goal is to build fluency now: researching, designing, prototyping, revising, and learning how to direct AI with judgment.

My academic path through Business and Information Systems gives me a practical foundation for thinking about technology not only as code, but as a system for solving problems and shaping experiences.

06 / AI Orchestration

AI is not the author. It is the instrument.

My workflow treats AI as a tool for acceleration, structure, and critique. The important skill is orchestration: knowing what to ask, what to reject, what to revise, and how to turn raw output into a finished digital product.

1

Research

I use AI to explore professional roles, tools, examples, design patterns, and expectations before building.

2

Strategy

I define the message, audience, structure, and purpose of the project before focusing on visuals.

3

Design

I use AI to test visual directions, page structures, copy options, and interaction ideas.

4

Build

I turn the plan into a working website, prototype, or structured digital product.

5

Critique

I use AI and human judgment to identify weak sections, unclear writing, design problems, and missing logic.

6

Refine

I revise the final product so it feels coherent, believable, intentional, and professionally grounded.
